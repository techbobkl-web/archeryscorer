Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the ArcheryScorer: Arrow Log app (the "App") for iOS and iPadOS.

Summary

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any data of any kind, including:

  • No personal information (name, email, location, contacts, photos)
  • No usage analytics or diagnostics
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking
  • No account creation or sign-in of any kind

Data Storage

All scores and round history you enter are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, and we have no ability to access it. Deleting the App permanently deletes its data.

Third Parties

The App contains no third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs, and it makes no network connections.

Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no data from anyone, it likewise collects no data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), consistent with COPPA, GDPR and other applicable privacy laws.

Changes to This Policy

If the App's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated here before the change takes effect.
